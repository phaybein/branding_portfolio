Color Theory For Developers That Are In Need Of Help With Design

Who knew that the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles were a great way to show Color Theory. Developers looking for some help regarding color theory for design then this is for you. Understanding color theory is a significant role for developers and this video makes the concepts straightforward for us to understand and also remember. Every time you see the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles you will now think of color theory. Who knew that color theory psychology was so embedded in the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and why they worked so well together.

Louisa Barret goes over color theory terms, the color theory wheel and you can even say this is color theory 101 for us programmers. Louisa Barrett talk is phenomenal and I hope you enjoy it just as much as I did. Shout out to those at Keep Ruby Weird 2018 for this fantastic video.

Here is some background information about Louisa Barrett. Louisa is the Director of the Front-End Engineering program at the Turing School of Software and Design. She is the former director of Colorado for Women Who Code and past chapter leader for Girl Develop It Denver/Boulder. She began her career as an illustrator/graphic designer, and a passion for understanding people lead her to programme.
